About

Hello! My name is Qiaoyi, English name is Joy, and I’m a UI/UX designer driven by a passion for creating user-friendly and visually engaging designs.
With over three years of experience in the field, I’ve honed my skills in design thinking, prototyping, and user research, working with both startups and established companies.
I believe that great design not only solves problems but also tells a story, connecting users to brands in meaningful ways.
I’m constantly exploring new tools and techniques to elevate my work and aim to contribute to projects that inspire and make a difference.

What I Do

Interaction Design

I specialize in crafting intuitive user experiences that seamlessly connect users with digital products. By focusing on usability and accessibility, I ensure every interaction feels natural and effortless.

Graphic Design

I bring ideas to life through visually compelling designs that captivate audiences. From branding to marketing materials, I create designs that are both functional and beautiful.

Motion Design

I create dynamic animations and transitions that enhance storytelling and elevate user engagement. My motion designs aim to bring energy and personality to every project.

Font Development

I design custom typefaces and font styles that align with brand identities and enhance readability. Every font I create is tailored to fit both aesthetic and functional needs.
